Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any special packages for couples who wish to have a wedding in Hawaii?

Yes, depending on the time of the year we offer different packages to allow for the newly weds to have a wonderful time. Some packages include catering, 5-star dining, and also an ocean view of the beach. The wedding venue can be customized, we will take into consideration the requests that the newly weds wish to have in their wedding.

What are the forms of transportation?

There are many forms of transportation, which includes free rides from our company's shuttle, we proudly offer free shuttle rides to specific destinations for our customers. Car rentals are more suitable when traveling around the islands, as the transportation from each attraction is limited.
